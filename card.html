Blue Whale

The blue whale is the largest animal known to have ever lived.

  • Scientific Name: Balaenoptera musculus
  • Average Length: 80.5 feet
  • Average Lifespan: At least 80 years
  • Habitat: Every ocean

The blue whale (Balaenoptera musculus) is a marine mammal belonging to the baleen whale parvorder, Mysticeti. At up to 98 ft in length and with a maximum recorded weight of 190 short tons, it is the largest animal known to have ever existed.
